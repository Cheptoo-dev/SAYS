    HISTORY OF KERICHO DIOCESE

    History

    Kericho Diocese was established as a Diocese from the Diocese of Nakuru on December 5th, 1995. The Diocese has 48 parishes with various religious institutions, congregations, schools, hospitals, health centers, projects and programmes.

    Past and Present Bishops

    Alfred Kipkoech Arap Rotich

    Appointed on 14th Dec 2019

    Emmanuel Okombo Wandera

    Appointed on 22 Mar 2003 - Retired on 14 Dec 2019

    Philip Arnold Subira Anyolo

    Appointed on 6 Dec 1995 - Appointed on 22 Mar 2003 as the Bishop of Homa Bay
