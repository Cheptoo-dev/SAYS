    SAYS LEDERSHIP

    Finance Administrator

    Rev. Fr. Kinyanjui

    The Finance Administrator heads the Finance office of the Diocese. The mandate of the office is to oversee the overall financial management of the Diocese of Kericho. Through this office, development strategies and projects are financed.

    Vocational Director/ Youth Chaplain

    Rev. Fr. Moses Langat

    The vocations office is tasked with the coordination of vocation animators and activities within the Diocese. The office also oversees the animation committees at the Diocesan, Deanery and parish levels. These committees comprise of priests, religious and the laity. Under the vocations office, open days for missionary and vocational animation are laid out in the General year plan of the church where all vocations are promoted.The youth office is the central coordinating office of all the youth activities in the Catholic Diocese of Kericho. The youth chaplain, in liaison with the Parish priests, Diocesan Youth Executive, and youth leadership at the Parish, and Deanery levels, works to promote spiritual and social formation for youths at all ages and levels of education in the Catholic Diocese of Kericho. The Chaplain is aided in his work by the Youth Diocesan Executive.

    Asst. Vocations Director/Youth Chaplain

    Rev. Fr. Geoffrey Bii

    The priest serves together with the vocations director and Youth Chaplain and assists the director in both offices.
